What our Students Say

Real Quotes from Real Students

I really love robotics because it gives me a chance to meet so many different people and learn so many different things. I'm in robotics because I simply love it. Not only does it provide great life lessons but it provides some of my best memories.

I love the joy of making things with my own hands, something that I helped design, build and test. It is really different from school because I feel that school can really be a plug and chug kinda thing where you don't really get to innovate.

What I love about robotics is the teamwork. We not only learn the many skills needed to become a programmer, engineer, or mechanic, we learn the skills to be team members, diligent workers, and leaders.

Robotics is fun because the task of creating and programming a robot is a form of expression akin to art that relatively few will undertake during their lifetimes.

Robotics not only introduces me to the world of engineering, but it teaches me how to work as part of a team and get things done by a deadline, no matter what problems arise.

It's great that we are allowed to try things and occasionally fail at them. The mentors give us suggestions, but we ultimately decide our success.
